Here are the tips:

Focus on whole foods:
Prioritize whole, unprocessed foods like vegetables, fruits, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. These foods provide essential nutrients, fiber, and satiety, making them a great starting point for a healthy diet.

Watch portion sizes:
Pay attention to the amount of food you consume in one sitting. Eat until you're satisfied, not stuffed. Use smaller plates and bowls to help control your portions and avoid overeating.

Stay hydrated:
Drink plenty of water throughout the day to help control hunger, boost energy, and support overall health. Aim for at least eight cups (64 ounces) of water daily, and limit sugary drinks like soda and juice.
